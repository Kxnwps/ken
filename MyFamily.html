Macasinag Family

My family is small but happy and close-knit. My dad works at Honda Cars Makati as a collector messenger, while my mom is an online seller. I have a younger sibling who's in their first year of high school. We were raised to be disciplined and respectful towards our elders. When it comes to household chores, we each have our own responsibilities, and we all eat dinner together when my dad comes home. My parents are hardworking and kind. Though we may not be wealthy, we are rich in love, and that's something I'm proud of.
